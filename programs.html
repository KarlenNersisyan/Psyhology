Psychology and Sociology courses

Secure your career in social sciences with a degree from Arden University


  • Our psychology and sociology degrees are designed to explore human behaviors across two key areas of social science. The courses offered by Arden University prepare you with a range of transferable skills that employers from a wide range of organisations will value.
  • Arden’s bachelor’s and master’s in psychology are both accredited by The British Pyschological Society, and all our psychology and social science degrees are internationally recognised.
  • There are several combined degree offerings for students who want to explore additional social sciences such as law and criminology, as well as combinations with other disciplines such as business and human resource management. This range of available programmes ensures you can choose an option that best suits your interests and career aspirations.
  • Forensic — Applying psychology to investigate criminal behaviors
  • At Arden, our psychology and sociology syllabuses are updated regularly, to ensure that what our students learn, in entrenched in the here and now.

Choose how you want to learn


  • At Arden University, we offer you the opportunity to study your way. Want to study your course entirely online from anywhere in the world? Then our online learning courses offer you the chance to do just that. Plus, you’ll have the flexibility to create your own study schedule that fits around your personal and professional life.
  • If you’d prefer to attend lectures and tutorials at one of our study centres in Manchester, London or Berlin, then our blended learning courses  in two days a week of face-to-face tuition with your course tutor and classmates, complemented by around 25 hours of online study.
  • Whichever route you choose, you can continue to work alongside your studies, either full or part-time, to ensure that you don’t press pause while achieving your qualifications. With Arden you don't need to give up work or any other commitments to gain an internationally recognized qualification that can take you places.

What can I study?


Bachelor’s courses in psychology and sociology

  • If you’re just at the start of your career, our psychology and sociology bachelor’s courses provide a comprehensive overview of the core skills you’ll need to get your professional life headed in the right direction. If you are interested in why the individual or a group behaves the way they do then these programmes will provide an engaging academic experience.
  • You’ll be surprised what career paths a social sciences degree can take you down. The skills you gain such as effective written communication skills and the analysis and evaluation of different forms of data can lead to careers in marketing, politics, and social work, to name a few!
